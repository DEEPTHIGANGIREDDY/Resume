Awards

Restaurant of the Year Award 2022

We're extremely glad to announce that Paradise Biryani has been recognized as the Best Restaurant 2022 by Restaurant Awards 2022, Hyderabad Edition. Authentic and timeless, we are delighted to have been part of your lives and a part of Hyderabad Since 1953.

Three cheers!

It rained awards on Paradise this month. First, we entered into the Limca Book of Records for "Most Biyani Servings Sold in a Year". Then, at the Asia Food Congress, Paradise received the prestigious "Restaurant Serving the Best Biryani" award. Our Chairman, Mr. Ali Hemati won the Lifetime Achievement award at the same event.

Times Food & Nightlife Award 2018

At the Times Food & Nightlife Awards 2018, Paradise Food Court walked away with the "Swiggy Public Choice for Biryani - Delivery" award. This honour stands as a testament to our efforts to consistently serve the world's favourite Biryani. We thank you all for your love.

TSHA Award 2017

The prestigious Wall Street Journal rated Paradise Biryani as the "Best Hyderabadi Biryani" and the Telangana State Hotels Association (TSHA) honoured us with the "Pride of Hyderabad" award. We thank all our guests and thousands of Paradise employees who made this happen.

GHMC Award 2017

Paradise won the "Outstanding Contribution to Swachhata hi Seva" award from GHMC. As an organisation, we have always been committed to hygiene and cleanliness and this award encourages us to do better.

Careers

Work Philosophy

Paradise is not only one of the most-admired food brands; it is also a great place to build a successful career. Our people are our magic ingredient, and more than anything, they help us deliver the quintessential Paradise experience.Every day, they bring their boundless energy, warmth and talent to delight our guests with great cuisine and service, treating each guest as unique and special.

Every hospitality business talks about hiring “nice” people. For us, this all-purpose adjective means three clear personality traits: high energy, natural inclination for guest service, and a sharp presence of mind. In an environment where a minor fumble can make or break an experience, it is important that you think on your feet, pay close attention to guest needs and deliver excellent service.

For our kitchen staff, we look for people who are passionate about great food. Our chefs have an ideal blend of respect for tradition and a desire to innovate. If you are interested in building a career with one of the fastest-growing Food & Hospitality brands, please browse through our current openings and apply for a suitable position.
